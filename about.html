Hello, I'm Ziye Zhang, an architect with a strong interest in small - scale architectural design, heritage conservation, and adaptive reuse.

I am currently seeking opportunities to contribute my skills to architectural firms in Europe (UK) and China (Hong Kong & Macau) that specialize in heritage building renovation and material - driven design. My approach emphasizes narrative-driven architecture, craftsmanship, and contextual sensitivity, aiming to transform spaces in a way that harmonizes tradition with modernity.

Through my portfolio, I showcase projects that reflect conceptual depth, technical execution, and thoughtful integration of historical and cultural contexts. I believe that architecture is not just about creating new structures, but about reinterpreting and revitalizing the existing fabric of our built environment.

Let’s connect and explore new possibilities in design！
